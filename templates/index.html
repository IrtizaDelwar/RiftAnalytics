Welcome to Rift Analytics!

Rift Analytics is a web application for the game League of Legends. On this web application we display various data and analytics for the game.

Features:

Ultimate Bravery

Are you ready for a challenge? Check out ultimate bravery! Click on the challenge button and follow the Champion, Summoner Spell, and Item Rules in your next match on Summoner's Rift.

Free Champion Rotation

Are you looking for a new champion to play? Check out the free champion rotation. Once per week Riot releases free champions for everyone to try out. Give it a go, you may find your perfect champion.

Your Profile

Take a look at how you are currently doing. Here you can view your solo rank, masteries, champion statistics, match history, and much more!
